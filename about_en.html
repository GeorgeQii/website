About Us

PleasantTech Ventures Co., Ltd. was established on February 3, 2023, with a registered capital of 500,000 RMB. The legal representative is Qi Ze Zhong, and the unified social credit code is 91110119MAC8PAYA8Y.

Our company is located at Building E, No. 387, Changcheng Road, Zhongguancun Yanqing Park, Yanqing District, Beijing, and belongs to the industry of technology promotion and application services.

Since its establishment, we have been committed to the promotion and application of technology services, providing comprehensive technical support and services. Our team consists of a group of passionate and innovative professionals who, with their extensive industry experience and profound technical knowledge, have provided high-quality solutions to our clients.

History: Since its inception, PleasantTech Ventures has always focused on customer-centricity and innovation-driven development, continuously expanding its business scope and improving service quality. Our history has witnessed the transformation of the company from a small-scale technology service company into a diversified technology service provider.

Vision: Our vision is to become a leader in technology promotion and application services, creating value for customers and contributing to society through continuous technological innovation and excellent service. We are dedicated to building an open, cooperative, and win-win technology service ecosystem, promoting the deep integration of technology and various industries, and advancing social progress and development.
